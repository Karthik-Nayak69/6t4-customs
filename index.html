02 / WORKSHOP CAPABILITIES

Performance Services

01

Performance Tuning

Complete engine balancing, throttle body synchronization, dynamic map corrections, and high-performance component matching to optimize power curves across all RPM ranges.

02

ECU Remapping

Custom calibration files adjusted for local fuel quality and ambient Hyderabad temperatures. Delivers cleaner throttle transitions, removes factory restrictions, and integrates custom quickshifter tolerances.

03

General Servicing

Technical maintenance including valve clearance adjustments, throttle body de-carbonization, vacuum sync, cooling system flushes, and Maxima synthetic racing lubricants.

04

Suspension Setup

Custom suspension geometry tuning. We measure and adjust rider sag, compression damping, rebound speed, and spring preloads tailored precisely to street or track targets.

05

Engine Swaps & Rebuilds

Internal mechanical blueprinting. Crank balancing, piston clearance calculations, cylinder head porting, valve seat grinding, and complete race-spec engine swaps.

06

Custom Paint & Restoration

Premium cosmetic execution. High-temperature ceramic coatings, custom frame powder coating, composite body panel repairs, and wet-look polyurethane clear coats.

07

Headlight Upgrades

Integration of high-illumination projector systems, custom housings like Matt Gecko systems, and heavy-duty wiring harnesses with waterproof relay terminals.

08

Safety & Protection

Strategic fitment of R&G engine case protectors, frame sliders, exhaust guards, carbon fiber tank protectors, and marine-grade stainless hardware.

09

Performance Parts

Professional sourcing and installation of full titanium exhaust arrays, lightweight race rearsets, Brembo calipers, Master cylinders, and quick-shifter units.

10

Advanced Diagnostics

Full digital system sweeps, sensor integrity mapping, voltage draw diagnostics, and CAN bus system troubleshooting to eliminate electrical gremlins.

11

Track Preparation

Technical preparation complying with race regulations: lock-wiring drain plugs, coolant flush to pure water, removal of side stands, and installing performance slicks.

03 / PERFORMANCE PIPELINE

The Workshop Experience

01

Inspection

Visual and physical alignment check. We inspect key frame structures, fluid levels, and overall chassis stability.

02

Diagnosis

Advanced ECU scans, electrical leak profiling, and physical mechanical checkups to establish baseline limits.

03

Consultation

We compile a technical report, review the upgrade map with you, list components, and secure target parameters.

04

Installation

Tear-down and assembly in our surgical workshop. All bolts are mapped and torqued strictly to manufacturer specs.

05

Testing

System diagnostic check, static warm-up heat cycles, and test-runs under load to verify structural modifications.

06

Delivery

Bike handover accompanied by full diagnostic reports, technical clearance log sheet, and torque checks certificate.
